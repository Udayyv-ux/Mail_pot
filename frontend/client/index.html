How to Use Sheetx.io

Everything you need to know to automate your email outreach.

1 Connect Your Google Account

To send emails on your behalf, we need permission to connect to your Gmail account. We only request the minimum permissions required to send emails and read the Google Sheets you explicitly share with us.

  • Go to the Settings tab.
  • Click the "Connect Google Workspace" button.
  • Grant the required permissions. Make sure to check the box to allow sending emails!

2 Create a Template

Define the core message you want to send. Our AI will automatically personalize it for each lead based on their data.

  • Go to the Templates tab.
  • Click "+ Create Template".
  • Write your subject line and email body. You can use variables like {{Name}} or {{Company}} if your sheet has those columns.
  • Save the template.

3 Launch a Campaign

Link a Google Sheet containing your leads to a Template, and start sending.

  • Go to the Campaigns tab and click "+ New Campaign".
  • Select the Template you created.
  • Paste the URL of your Google Sheet containing the leads.
    Note: The sheet must be "Anyone with the link can view" or explicitly shared with your connected Google account.
  • Map the columns (e.g. tell the system which column has the Email address).
  • Click Start Campaign. The engine will process rows sequentially.
